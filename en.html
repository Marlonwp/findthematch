About the Band

Find The Match is an alternative indie band formed in southern Brazil. The project was born from friendship and the gathering of different life stories around music.

Rodrigo Noro (vocals) comes from the grunge school, with a striking and original voice — his hoarse, energetic tone brings personality to the songs.

Fabio Heck (guitar) has roots in hardcore, delivering intense riffs and blazing solos that elevate every track.

Matteo (bass) also carries hardcore influences, with a technical approach and precision that sustain the band's groove.

Marlon Werner (guitar and drums) is the most cerebral and self-taught member, moving through different styles and musical references, from rock classics to contemporary alternative sounds.

The first single, Live in Peace, will be available on digital platforms in August 2025.
