About the Band

Find The Match is an alternative indie band formed in southern Brazil. The project was born from friendship and the coming together of different life stories around music.

Rodrigo Noro (vocals) comes from the grunge scene, with a striking and original voice — his raspy, energetic tone gives the songs their personality.

Fabio Heck (guitar) has hardcore roots, delivering intense riffs and blazing solos that elevate each track.

Matteo (bass) also brings hardcore influences, with a technical groove and precision that sustain the band's sound.

Marlon Werner (guitar and drums) is the most cerebral and self-taught member, blending different styles and musical references, from classic rock to contemporary alternative.

The first single, Live in Peace, will be available on digital platforms in August 2025.
